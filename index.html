about

Beartooth Mountain Conference is a gathering of AA and Al-Anon participants exploring the joy of living in an atmosphere of fun and fellowship. This gathering will enhance your spiritual growth through speakers with quality sobriety and recovery. "This is an experience you will not want to miss!"

July 19-21, 2025 will change your life! This inspirational and life-changing weekend features activities, events and speakers to help you experience the joy of living!

The Conference will held at Beartooth Christian Camp. You will be swept away by the wonder of nature and inspired by the fellowship. Join us to meet old friends, and make new ones as you experience this unique event.

Rustic facilities and fun-filled events await you in the pristine beauty of Montana. Top-notch speakers, special activities, and an optional golf tournament make this a weekend to remember for life.
